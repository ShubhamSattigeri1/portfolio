Business Canvas

Instantly generate a QR code that, when scanned, shares their portfolio and contact info.
Easy sharing for networking, events, job fairs, and professional meetings.

Students and job seekers.
Professionals attending conferences.
Colleges/universities for campus placements.
Recruiters and HR professionals.

Web application (Streamlit-based).

Social media and campus partnerships.

Self-service registration.
Automated QR code generation.
Email/SMS support for premium users.
