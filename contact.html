Contact

This page helps visitors reach the site owner with questions about the tool, report broken links, flag privacy concerns, or suggest new educational content. Clear contact details are also an important trust signal for publisher-quality websites.

Publish a real, monitored contact email on this page before submitting the site for advertising review. A placeholder address is not a trustworthy contact method.

Before you publish this site

  • Replace placeholder contact information with a monitored email address or support form.
  • Make sure the contact method belongs to the real site owner or publisher.
  • Keep response details, business identity, and site purpose consistent across trust pages.

Good reasons to reach out

  • You found a broken QR generation flow or a bad external link.
  • You have a privacy or policy question about how the generator works.
  • You want to suggest a new guide for students, developers, or job seekers.
  • You noticed confusing content that should be corrected for visitors.

Public information reminder

This site is built for public-facing portfolio details only. Please do not include passwords, government IDs, full home addresses, or any sensitive personal information in messages or generated profile links.
