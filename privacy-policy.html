Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Portfolio QR Generator handles information provided through the site. The tool is intended for public portfolio and networking use, so users should only enter details they are comfortable sharing publicly. This policy applies to users of all ages, including children under 13.

Site Owner: Portfolio-QR-Generator | Contact: tweryp7@gmail.com

Information you choose to enter

When using the generator, you may provide a first name, surname, college, city, GitHub profile URL, LinkedIn profile URL, and optionally a mobile number. These values are used to create a profile link and a QR code that points to that link.

Because the generated link contains profile data in the URL, that information should be treated as public once shared. If you choose to include a phone number, it should also be treated as public profile information. Do not use this tool for passwords, banking details, government identification numbers, medical information, or other sensitive data.

How the site works

The current version of the site does not require account registration and does not present itself as a private storage platform. The core experience is based on generating a link from the information you enter during your session.

Technical services involved in delivering a website may still process standard request data, such as browser type, IP address, referring page, and device information, subject to the policies of your browser, hosting provider, analytics tools, or advertising partners.

Third-party services and QR generation

QR images may be requested from a third-party QR generation service. When that happens, the service may receive technical request information necessary to deliver the image. External profile links such as GitHub and LinkedIn are also governed by the privacy policies of those third-party platforms.

Google AdSense and cookies

This site may use Google AdSense or other advertising services to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this site or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, and may review additional information at Google's advertising technologies page.

If third-party vendors or ad networks are used, those partners may also use cookies to measure performance and personalize advertising where permitted by law. Visitors should review the privacy policies of those services for more detail.

You can also review how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Publisher transparency and data minimization

The site is intended for lawful, public-facing portfolio sharing. It should not be used to publish deceptive, unlawful, infringing, harmful, or sensitive information. Profile links are limited to GitHub and LinkedIn destinations to reduce misleading-link abuse.

This page describes the current behavior of the site. If analytics, consent tools, or additional advertising partners are added later, this policy should be updated before those changes go live.

Your choices

  • Use only public-facing profile information in generated links.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block or clear cookies if you prefer.
  • Do not share a QR code until you have reviewed the link it opens.
  • Only use genuine GitHub and LinkedIn profile URLs in the form.
  • Regenerate your QR code if your public information changes.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) Compliance

Important: This site is accessible to users of all ages, including children under 13. The following protections apply:

  • No interest-based advertising: If we detect users under 13, no personalized, targeted, or interest-based ads will be shown to them.
  • No targeted ads: We do not collect or use personal information from users under 13 to build audience segments or conduct retargeting.
  • No tracking for behavioral profiling: Cookies and similar technologies will not be used to track users under 13 for ad personalization.
  • Parental notice: If you are a parent or guardian and your child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us immediately at tweryp7@gmail.com.
  • Data minimization: The site only collects information that users voluntarily enter during QR generation (name, college, city, GitHub, LinkedIn URLs). No hidden tracking or data harvesting occurs.

Users under 13 should only enter information they are comfortable sharing publicly and should not include phone numbers or sensitive details.
